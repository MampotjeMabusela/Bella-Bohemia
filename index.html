Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you book your escape.

What should I wear or bring?

Wear comfortable clothing. We provide towels and robes for treatments. For overnight stays, bring an overnight bag and any personal items you need. Leave jewellery at home if possible.

Are Tranquil Treat Days only Monday – Thursday?

Yes, the 60 min and 90 min combo packages are a Monday – Thursday special. Message us on WhatsApp to check availability for your preferred date.

Can I add hot stones to my massage?

Yes! Hot stone add-on is available for the overnight package at R100 per person. Mention it when you book.

Is it suitable if I'm pregnant or have allergies?

Please tell us about any medical conditions, allergies, or pregnancy when booking. We'll tailor your treatment or advise if rescheduling is needed for your safety.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations more than 7 days before arrival receive a full refund. See our full policy on the Terms & Conditions page.

How do I book?

The fastest way is WhatsApp — tap any "Book on WhatsApp" button, or fill in the quote form and send your details. We typically respond within a few hours.
